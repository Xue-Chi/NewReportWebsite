Requirements

Project background

Older people who live on their own, are especially vulnerable to loneliness and social isolation and it can have a serious effect on health.

Requirements gathering

The hardware and software we prefer to choose is ‘Oculus Quest’ and ‘Unity platform’, ‘Oculus Quest’ is provided by the department, and we took away that on the first day of December.

We did not take any survey. This is because that we were invited to attend a VR lab session held by Teaching assistants to familiarize ourselves with the capabilities of each VR kit. We were able to thoroughly learn about the capabilities of various devices and select the appropriate kit for our project.

Project goals

The aim is to recreate comforting VR scenes where the users especially the old can meet their family member or friends in game and to remove their loneliness and isolation by providing multiple avatars, sceneries and also NPC.
